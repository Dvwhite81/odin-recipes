Ingredients

  • Meat:

    Sweet Italian sausage, lean ground beef

  • Onion and Garlic:

    An onion and two cloves of garlic

  • Tomato Products:

    One can of crushed tomatoes, two cans of tomato sauce, and two cans of tomato paste

  • Sugar:

    Two tablespoons of white sugar

  • Spices:

    Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper

  • Noodles:

    Store-bought or homemade lasagna noodles

  • Cheeses:

    Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese

  • Egg:

    To bind the ricotta

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
